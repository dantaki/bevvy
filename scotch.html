Islay

The island of Islay is home to some of the most distinctive and well known Scottish whiskies. Marked by the heavy use of peat, these scotches express a smoky marine essence, transporting you to a 17th century sailing ship lost in a foggy sweet barely seascape.

Flagship distilleries include:

  • Lagavulin
  • Laphroaig
