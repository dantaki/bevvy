Martini

Delicate cocktail rich with botanical flavors from the gin and dry vermouth. Express the lemon rind over the drink, avoid rubbing it around the rim. Serve cold as a penguin's dick.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1oz Dry Vermouth
  • Dash Orange Bitters
  • Serve with a lemon twist

Pompeii

Own creation. Basic martini with limoncello. Express lemon peel over the drink. Wife approved. Taste is botanically light with echoes of lemonade without the fizz. Great after Thanksgiving better on after a hot day. Maybe consider champagne or seltzer.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1oz Dry Vermouth
  • 1/2 oz Limoncello
  • Dash Orange Bitters
  • Serve with a lemon twist

Spring Forward

Own creation. Cardamom infused martini with elderflower liqueur. Express lemon peel over the drink. Has a honey taste with floral notes. Nectar taste. Wife approved greatly, tastes like the coming of spring.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1oz Dry Vermouth
  • 1oz St. Germain Elderflower Liqueur
  • Half a dropper of cardamom bitters
  • Dash Orange Bitters to taste
  • Serve with a lemon twist

Martinez

Cocktail Codex p86. Express lemon peel over the drink. Book says to set on edge of the glass. I like to serve with a maraschino cherry.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 1tsp Maraschino liqueur
  • 2 dashes Orange Bitters
  • Serve with a lemon twist (or maraschino cherry)

Lusty Lady

Recipe from Scrappy's Bitters. Shake until frothy and serve with a brandied cherry.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Lime juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple syrup
  • 1/8 oz Cranberry juice
  • 1 Egg white
  • 2-3 dashes Lavender Bitters
  • Serve with brandied cherries
