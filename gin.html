Martini

Delicate cocktail rich with botanical flavors from the gin and dry vermouth. Express the lemon rind over the drink, avoid rubbing it around the rim. Serve cold as a penguin's dick.

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1oz Dry Vermouth
  • Dash Orange Bitters
  • Serve with a lemon twist
